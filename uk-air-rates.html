CargoGuide-Style Search, Built for UK Forwarders

Platforms like CargoGuide give large forwarders a pre-filled global rate database through carrier partnerships. Most UK forwarders still work from PDF rate sheets emailed by airlines and GSAs every week.

Our free UK Air Rate Search closes that gap: we ingest those tariff PDFs, extract the lanes and weight breaks, and give you a fast search — origin, destination, weight — across every published UK export rate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the same as CargoGuide?

CargoGuide (now part of WiseTech) provides a large pre-integrated global rate database for subscribed forwarders. Our tool is free, UK-focused, and built from published tariff PDFs — ideal for forwarders who don't have a CargoGuide subscription.

Are the rates guaranteed?

No — all results are indicative based on published tariff sheets. Surcharges, capacity, and contract terms may vary. Always confirm with your airline or GSA before booking.

Can I upload our own rate PDFs?

Public uploads are curated by the LogistiCore team while we build coverage. If you want private rate management inside your own tenant, that's part of LogistiCore SaaS.
