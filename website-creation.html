Website Development Process

1. Discovery & Planning

We start with a detailed consultation to understand your business, target audience, and goals. We'll discuss:

  • Your services and unique selling points
  • Competitor analysis
  • Branding and design preferences
  • Required features and functionality
  • Content requirements

2. Design & Review

Our designers create mockups of your homepage and key pages. You'll have the opportunity to review and request revisions until you're 100% happy.

3. Development & Content

Once designs are approved, we build the website using modern web technologies. We can work with your existing content or help you craft compelling copy.

4. Testing & Launch

Before going live, we thoroughly test the site across different browsers and devices. Once you approve, we launch your new website and handle DNS setup.

5. Training & Support

We provide training on how to update your website (if using a CMS). Ongoing support packages are available for maintenance, updates, and enhancements.
