Invoice Data Extraction Tool

Upload a PDF invoice. Get an Excel file ready to import into your customs entry system.

Stop Keying Invoice Data By Hand

If you work in freight forwarding or customs brokerage, you know the pain: a supplier sends a commercial invoice as a PDF, and someone has to manually key every line item — descriptions, quantities, values, country of origin — into your customs entry system. It's slow, error-prone, and adds no value.

The LogistiCore Invoice Tool changes that. Upload your PDF invoice, and it automatically extracts all the data you need and exports it to a structured Excel spreadsheet — formatted and ready to upload directly into your customs entry system.

How It Works

Three steps. No manual keying. No errors.

📄

1. Upload Your PDF Invoice

Upload any commercial invoice in PDF format — supplier invoices, pro-forma invoices, or packing lists. The tool reads the document automatically.

⚡

2. Data Extracted Automatically

The tool identifies and extracts goods descriptions, quantities, unit values, total values, countries of origin, and other key fields from every line item.

📊

3. Download Your Excel File

Download a structured Excel spreadsheet with your data mapped to the columns your customs entry system expects — ready to upload immediately.

What the Tool Extracts

🏷️

Goods Descriptions

Full line-by-line goods descriptions extracted as they appear on the invoice, ready for your customs entry commodity lines.

🔢

Quantities & Units

Item quantities and units of measurement extracted per line — no more manually counting or transcribing from pages of invoice data.

💷

Values & Currencies

Unit prices, line totals, and invoice currency captured accurately — critical for correct customs valuation.

🌍

Country of Origin

Origin countries extracted per commodity line where declared on the invoice — essential for preference and tariff calculations.

📋

Invoice Header Data

Supplier name and address, invoice number, invoice date, buyer details, and Incoterms — all captured without manual input.

📦

Package Details

Number of packages, gross weights, and net weights where available — feeding directly into your shipment and declaration fields.

Frequently Asked Questions

What PDF formats does it support?

The tool works with standard text-based PDF invoices, including those generated by ERP systems, accounting software, and supplier portals. If the PDF contains selectable text, the tool can read it.

Which customs entry systems does the Excel output support?

The Excel output is formatted to match standard customs entry upload templates. We can configure the column mapping to match your specific system — whether you use ASM Sequoia, CNS, or another UK customs platform.

Can it handle invoices from different suppliers in different formats?

Yes. The tool is designed to handle varied invoice layouts. While performance can vary with unusual or complex layouts, it handles the vast majority of commercial invoice formats used in international trade.

Is the Invoice Tool included in LogistiCore SaaS?

Yes — it is available as part of the LogistiCore SaaS platform. It can also be accessed as a standalone tool for organisations that don't need the full freight management platform. Contact us to discuss options.

Is my invoice data kept secure and private?

Absolutely. Your invoice data is processed securely and never stored or shared. All data handling complies with UK GDPR requirements.
