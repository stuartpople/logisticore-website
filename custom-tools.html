Custom Tools & Business Automation

If a task is costing your team time, we can build a tool to solve it

The Possibilities Are Endless

Every business has tasks that eat time for no good reason — data being re-keyed from one system to another, reports built by hand every week, documents processed one by one, spreadsheets that need updating from a dozen sources. These aren't problems you have to live with.

At LogistiCore Technologies, we build bespoke software tools that eliminate exactly these kinds of problems. You describe the task — we build the tool. No off-the-shelf software that almost fits. A precise solution built for your specific process.

A Real Example: The Invoice Tool

Our Invoice Data Extraction Tool is a perfect example of this approach. Customs brokers and freight forwarders were spending 20–40 minutes manually keying every commercial invoice into their customs entry system. We built a tool that extracts all the data from the PDF automatically and exports a structured Excel file ready to upload — cutting that time to under 60 seconds.

What Kind of Tools Can We Build?

📄

Document Data Extraction

Extract structured data from PDFs, scanned documents, or emails — invoices, purchase orders, delivery notes, certificates, anything — and output it to Excel, a database, or another system.

🔄

Data Conversion & Transformation

Convert data between formats — CSV to XML, Excel to JSON, one system's export to another system's import format. Stop copying and pasting between platforms.

📊

Automated Reports & Dashboards

Reports that currently take hours to compile can be generated automatically — pulling from your existing data sources and formatting exactly how you need them.

🔗

API Integrations & Connectors

Connect systems that don't talk to each other. We build integrations between your existing platforms — ERP, CRM, freight systems, government portals, carrier APIs.

✉️

Email & Notification Automation

Automated emails triggered by events in your business — shipment status updates, document requests, deadline reminders, approval workflows. Set it up once, let it run.

🗄️

Data Validation & Cleansing Tools

Tools that check your data for errors, inconsistencies, or missing fields before it reaches a downstream system — catching problems before they cause delays or penalties.

📋

Form & Checklist Automation

Replace manual paper or PDF-based forms and checklists with digital tools that capture data, enforce rules, and route information to the right people automatically.

🧮

Calculation & Pricing Tools

Complex calculations — tariff classifications, freight rate calculators, duty estimators, costing models — built as clean, shareable tools your team or clients can use.

Web Scraping & Data Gathering

Automatically collect data from websites, government portals, or public databases — exchange rates, tariff codes, compliance updates — and feed it into your processes.
